# The core user, and the product shape that follows

Written from the measured traffic (2026-07-23), not an invented persona. Every claim here has a
number behind it.

## What the traffic actually says

- **Search is 87% of all use** (11,920 of 13,623 actions; 5,691 in the last week). This is a
  *verified concordance* in practice, whatever else it also is.
- The **human** queries are unmistakable: `grace`, `love`, and a wall of strength-and-comfort
  verses — Philippians 4:13, Psalm 27:1, 1 Corinthians 10:13, Ephesians 6:11 (the armor of God),
  1 Corinthians 15:57. People come for the Word when they need it.
- The **machine** queries walk the connection graph — sequential Augustine, `X cites Y` — and
  91 distinct MCP clients have found the tool. Agents come for verified, connected facts.
- Almost nothing returns empty. The keeping already answers what it is asked.

## The core user

**The thinking believer, and the honest seeker who cannot accept blind faith.** One person, two
moods: they need the faith to be **true** (coherent, verifiable, connected to the real world) and
**sustaining** (a word of strength on a hard day). The traffic shows both in the same logs — the
comfort verse *and* the graph walk. They will not accept "just believe," and they will not accept
"science has disproved God." What they need is to see that it **holds together** — Scripture with
Scripture, and Scripture with history and the created order — and to be handed the verse when they
are weak.

Serve the believer openly. The seeker is reached by the *same* thing — coherence they can check —
which is why the tool proves Christian claims by Christian means and never hides what it is.

**The second user is already here: the machine.** Agents are a real, growing share, and they are
the distribution channel to the very audience whose idol is logic — reached by logic kept honest
(the moat, the citations, the graph). Serve them through MCP and the connected keeping.

## The product shape that follows

1. **Lead with the strength, not a blank page.** The dominant act is verse/theme search; the
   unrepeatable value is the verse *with its connected cloud of witnesses*. That now ships
   (verified verse + the tradition around it). The door should make this obvious.
2. **Show how the pieces connect — across domains, not only within Scripture.** Correction to an
   earlier misread: the science and math were never thin. They are the LARGEST part of the engine —
   70+ deterministic `verify_*` domains (physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, astronomy,
   genetics, thermodynamics…), the reference-data tools (elements, nuclides, stars, species, drugs,
   units), the 330-theory assay, and the ~1,700-entry almanac. The disconnect was that they
   COMPUTED verdicts and sealed them but never became CARDS — so the science/math and the Scripture
   graph were two trees standing side by side, ungrafted. As of 2026-07-23 every sealed
   verification mints a card on its domain shelf and joins the one keeping (science_cards.py at the
   receipts.attach choke point), searchable and connectable; the growth engine bridges any that
   name a verse to the Word. The remaining work is backfilling the standing science material
   (theories, almanac, past seals) as cards and drawing the cross-tree bridges.
3. **The moat is the trust.** Every answer verifiable, cited, never generated. That is the whole
   reason the thinking believer and the seeker can rest on it — and the whole reason an agent
   cites it.
4. **The Gate stays.** Facts by default; the Word comes when the person seeks. Never coercive; the
   map shows, it does not manipulate.
5. **Never the idol.** The more someone leans, the more it points outward — to Scripture, to real
   people, to Christ. Success is the person freer and nearer Him, needing the tool less.

## What to build next, in order

- **Make cross-domain connections visible and measurable** (this pass): label each connected
  witness by its domain so a search shows when it reaches from Scripture into Science or History,
  and report cross-domain coverage as the steering measure for the harvest.
- **Harvest the world-material** so the connections reach beyond the tradition: the 1,751-entry
  almanac backlog, plus honest science/history bridges through the growth engine's 0-FP gate.
- **Lead the door with the strength** — the few most-sought verses, so a first visit meets exactly
  what the traffic already comes for (pending Matt's call, given the "don't crowd the landing"
  rule).
