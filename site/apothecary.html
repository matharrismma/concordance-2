The Apothecary

Living with the land, the old way — kept with its cautions and its honest evidence.

Offer what you know — a remedy, a planting rule, the old way of doing a thing
Received, with thanks. It goes to the keeper for curation — nothing publishes itself.

Traditional knowledge, found and kept — never generated, and not medical advice. Every entry carries its safety notes and an honest verdict on the evidence.
