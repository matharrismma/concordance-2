The Coach

Learn to Read

A reading tutor that grows with the student — one step at a time, read aloud, at the child's pace. Every lesson is found and presented, never generated, and never a grade on a child. It remembers where you are, so the path never starts over. For a parent and child together.

The curriculum is the offline Read‑school teaching, ported verbatim. The engine finds, orders, and presents the lessons and reads them aloud; it does not generate them, and it will never grade, rank, or label a child. Progress is kept on this device — no account, no personal data.

Pronunciation is a synthesized guide (or Matt's voice when available), not a native speaker. A window to reading, not a wall. narrowhighway.org
