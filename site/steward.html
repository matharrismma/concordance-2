Stewarded

Steward your money

The honest arithmetic of a household — a budget, a savings rate, and cost destroyed (money you did not spend) — verified and sealed, a receipt for your money.

Steward shows and plans — it never moves your money. No buying, selling, transferring, paying, or investing (that stays your action, in your own bank). And no personalized investment advice — for that, see a licensed professional. A conduit, not your banker.

Cost destroyed

Money you kept — what a thing used to cost you (was) versus now.

Value returned as cost destroyed — money kept in your currency, not metered by us. Kill rent, not profit. Your details are stripped before anything is sent; the math is sealed so you can re-check it. Steward stewards; it does not transact.
