The receipt

Every verdict is content-addressed. Re-fetch it by its hash; the hash is the proof.

A seal is the permanent, tamper-evident record of a verification — the verdict, the worked trail, the anchors. Paste a content hash to retrieve it. If the bytes were altered, the hash would no longer match.

The trail is the trust: each step is machine-verified, and the seal lets anyone re-check it. The fuller witness is at narrowhighway.org.
