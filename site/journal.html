Kept

Your journal

Some of the best ideas come and go in a chat and are lost. Not here. Keep an idea, a writing, anything — it lives once and shows up wherever it belongs: by the day, and in whatever topics you give it. Your details are stripped before anything is sent.

Kept by date; the same entry also lives in each topic you give it — one card, many stacks.

One card lives once and appears in every stack it belongs to — the day, the topics, and (for a conversation) its thread. Seldom-used entries move to slower storage; nothing is thrown away. Conduit: your own words, kept verbatim, never generated.
