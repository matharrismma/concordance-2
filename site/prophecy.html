Signposts

How the world reached for Christ before He came — and points to Him still. Prophecy and cross-cultural signposts, each attributed to its source. A signpost, not a proof: the verdict is never a deterministic seal, and the destination is always Jesus Christ.

Each trace names its own sources (Plato's Republic, the Great Isaiah Scroll, the Shatapatha Brahmana, and more). Verdicts: CONCORDANT (real, points unmistakably to Christ), MIXED (real fragment, the tradition missed Him), STRUCTURAL — never HOLDS. Fulfillment is a witness, not a proof (1 John 4:1-3). Found and attributed, never generated. The engine points; it does not manufacture the fulfillment.
