# The Foundation

> "For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ." — 1 Corinthians 3:11

The foundation of this engine is the Bible. Not a feature, not a module, not an add-on that one
face installs and another omits — the **bedrock** the whole thing is built on and from. **Christ
is at the center of the architecture. We are not hiding anything.**

## Christ at the center — the mainspring

In a watch the power comes from the mainspring at the heart, and every wheel turns from the
center. Christ is the center of this architecture and the power that drives the train — "by him
all things consist" (Col 1:17), "upholding all things by the word of his power" (Heb 1:3). He is
not an absentee watchmaker who winds it and leaves; the mainspring is turning *now*. The
exhibition caseback is open: the `.org` shows the movement named, the `.com` shows it working.

## What "foundation" means here (load-bearing on every surface)

1. **The truth-commitment.** The engine serves truth because Truth is a Person (John 14:6). It
   hands a receipt, not a "trust me," because it answers to One who cannot lie.
2. **The disciplines.** Conduit, not source. Points to Christ, never stands in His place (an
   instrument, never an idol). Witness, not proof — verification witnesses; the Word is first.
   Decrease — success is the person leaving nearer the truth and needing the tool less (John 3:30).
3. **Scripture in the substrate.** The Word is foundational reference, not optional data.
4. **All truth is already His.** "In whom are hid all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge"
   (Col 2:3); "the fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge" (Prov 1:7). Math, physics, and
   logic are the order written into creation (Rom 2:14–15; Ps 24:1). The engine stands on this
   foundation whether or not a given surface names it.

## Reaching the world — two surfaces, one foundation

- **`.com` — the reach.** Meets the world in accessible, outward-facing language: truth,
  verification, a re-checkable receipt. It reaches the world where it is — **openly Christian-rooted,
  not hiding the faith** (the keeping is shared: the `.com` includes the religious cards too —
  operator, 2026-06-26: "the religious cards can be included"; "we are not hiding anything").
  This is Paul at the Areopagus (Acts 17) — reasoning from what they already accept, quoting their
  own poets, never moving the foundation. Speaking the hearer's tongue is not shame; it is love
  (1 Cor 9:19–22). "Secular" here means *accessible / for the world*, not *faith-scrubbed*.
- **`.org` — the witness.** The same engine with the foundation made explicit: Scripture, canon,
  theology, the Shepherd, the named Rock.

The faith is never the removable layer. If anything, the secular presentation is the outward
reach built on the rock. `.org` does not *add* the foundation — it *surfaces* it. `.com` does not
*remove* it — it stands on it, and openly links to it.

## The guardrail

1. **No bait-and-switch** — `.com` is a genuine truth tool, honest on both surfaces.
2. **The surface differs; the foundation never does** — the seal discipline and **zero
   false-positives** hold identically on both. Truth is His whether or not it is labeled.
3. **Never hidden in shame** (Mk 8:38) — the witness is full on `.org`, openly linked from `.com`.
4. **Decrease wins** (John 3:30).

In code: `src/concordance/config.py` — one `EngineConfig`, two `surface` values, one shared
foundation. The surface selects only what is *surfaced*, never whether the foundation exists.
